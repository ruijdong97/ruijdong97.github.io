About Me

I am Ruijiang Dong, a Ph.D. student in the Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning Group (TMLR), Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, The University of Melbourne, advised by Dr. Feng Liu, Prof. Jianzhong Qi, and Dr. Zesheng Ye.

My research focuses on vision-language models (VLMs), with a particular interest in efficient adaptation and prompt learning for large-scale models.

Prior to my Ph.D., I worked as a research assistant at the TMLR Lab at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), where I developed methods for source-free domain adaptation.
